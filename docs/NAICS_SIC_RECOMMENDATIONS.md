# NAICS/SIC Recommendations

Status: preliminary - verify before official registration.

These recommendations are starting points for ACoolECOSYSTEM positioning. Confirm with a qualified advisor, contracting specialist, or official registration resource before using them in formal filings.

## Preliminary NAICS Codes

- 541511 - Custom Computer Programming Services: useful for software development, civic platforms, dashboards, and workflow tools.
- 541512 - Computer Systems Design Services: useful for product architecture, systems planning, and implementation support.
- 541519 - Other Computer Related Services: useful for technical support, automation setup, and digital modernization.
- 541611 - Administrative Management and General Management Consulting Services: useful for operating strategy, workflow design, and business support.
- 541613 - Marketing Consulting Services: useful for outreach, campaign support, creator-commerce, and public engagement positioning.
- 541618 - Other Management Consulting Services: useful for civic modernization, program support, and implementation planning.
- 541690 - Other Scientific and Technical Consulting Services: useful for data, AI, and technical advisory services.
- 541720 - Research and Development in the Social Sciences and Humanities: useful for civic research, community intelligence, and policy-adjacent research.
- 611430 - Professional and Management Development Training: useful for AI training, workforce support, and entrepreneurship training.
- 519290 - Web Search Portals and All Other Information Services: useful for information platforms, knowledgebases, and civic resource hubs.
