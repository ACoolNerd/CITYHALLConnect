# Government Contracting Positioning

Position the ACoolECOSYSTEM as a premier small-business partner for local government and LA28-related initiatives.

## Key Focus Areas
1. **Digital Modernization**: Helping city departments transition from legacy systems to modern, AI-augmented workflows.
2. **Community Outreach**: Leveraging CITYHALLConnect to improve public engagement and transparency.
3. **Data Dashboards**: Providing real-time visualizations of city performance and public sentiment.
4. **AI Training**: Upskilling workforce and municipal staff on agentic tools.

- **Vendor Support**: Providing digital infrastructure for local vendors.
- **Small-Business Assistance**: Helping local creators and businesses navigate the digital requirements of major events.
- **Civic Navigation**: Real-time tools for residents and visitors during the games.
