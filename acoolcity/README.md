# ACooCITY: CITYHALLConnect Civic Intelligence Wiki

Made with LOVE by ACoolNERD with ACoolAI.

ACooCITY is the umbrella civic operating system for the ACoolAI civic-tech stack. It organizes city government, chambers, residents, public data, business support, procurement, grants, programs, Neighborhood Councils, and verified source records into one reusable intelligence system.

## Product family

- CITYHALLConnect - city government, council, departments, meetings, reports, elections and public services.
- CHAMBERConnect - chambers, business associations, economic coalitions, advocacy, events and membership flows.
- CITIZENConnect - resident services, issue reporting, public comment, civic education and public meetings.
- BUSINESSConnect - business licenses, permits, BusinessSource, capital access and technical assistance.
- COUNCILConnect - elected officials, districts, committees, voting records and agenda intelligence.
- NEIGHBORConnect - Neighborhood Councils, community boards, CIS tracking and local committees.
- DATAConnect - open data, annual reports, dashboards, civic indicators and source registry.
- GRANTConnect - public/private grants, nonprofit funding, eligibility and proposal workflows.
- PROCUREConnect - RFPs, RFQs, NIBs, bids, vendor readiness and LA28 opportunity tracking.
- PROGRAMConnect - public programs, benefits, workshops, city services and referral maps.
- BRANDConnect - official city branding, logos, seals, flags, public-use rules and design references.
- SOURCEConnect - Rights -> Disclosure -> Proof governance, citations and confidence scoring.
- AGENTConnect - ACoolAI agents that ingest, summarize, route, verify and prepare action items.

## Notion import path

Import the files in `/notion` as wiki pages and the CSV files in `/data` as databases. Use `/prompts` to run new city scrapes and `/figma` to build ACooCITY visual dashboards.

## Rule

Rights -> Disclosure -> Proof.

No owner, funding, political, endorsement or influence claim should be inferred. Every record must carry a source URL, scrape date, confidence score, rights status and review status.
