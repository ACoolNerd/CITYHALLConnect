# ACooCITY Operating Manual

Made with LOVE by ACoolNERD with ACoolAI.

## Definition

ACooCITY is the civic operating-system brand for CITYHALLConnect, CHAMBERConnect, CITIZENConnect and the full ACoolAI civic intelligence stack.

It answers five questions:

1. Who is who?
2. What does each city, chamber, council, program and agency do?
3. Where are the reports, meetings, elections, business resources and opportunities?
4. What should people or partners do next?
5. What is verified, uncertain or ready for review?

## Product family

- CITYHALLConnect: city government, council, departments, reports and elections.
- CHAMBERConnect: chambers, coalitions, business associations and events.
- CITIZENConnect: services, public participation and civic education.
- BUSINESSConnect: permits, licensing, capital access and business support.
- COUNCILConnect: elected officials, agendas, committees and district issues.
- NEIGHBORConnect: Neighborhood Councils, local boards and community signals.
- DATAConnect: reports, dashboards, open data and indicators.
- GRANTConnect: grants, eligibility and proposal workflows.
- PROCUREConnect: RFPs, RFQs, vendor readiness and public contracts.
- PROGRAMConnect: city programs, public services and referrals.
- BRANDConnect: city visual references, official assets and usage notes.
- SOURCEConnect: citations, proof, confidence scoring and QA.
- AGENTConnect: ACoolAI agents for ingestion, routing and review.

## Notion operating model

The Notion workspace is the control center. Every page explains a module. Every database stores records. Every record connects back to a source.

Core databases:

- Organizations.
- People and Roles.
- Programs and Services.
- Public Reports.
- Procurement Opportunities.
- Grants and Funding.
- Chambers and Coalitions.
- Neighborhood Councils.
- Sources.
- Actions and Outreach.

## Governance

Rights -> Disclosure -> Proof is the operating law.

Do not infer business owners, political relationships, funding commitments, endorsements or influence claims. Store uncertain records as Needs Verification.

## Build sequence

1. Import the module registry.
2. Create the source registry.
3. Import jurisdiction records.
4. Add organization profiles.
5. Add people and roles.
6. Add programs and services.
7. Add procurement, grants and reports.
8. Build Figma frames.
9. Activate ACoolAI agents.
10. Review before publication.
