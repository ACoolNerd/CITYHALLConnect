# CITYHALLConnect LA Economic Development Network

Made with LOVE by ACoolNERD with ACoolAI.

This package expands CITYHALLConnect from city-hall scraping into a regional economic-development intelligence layer for Los Angeles and the San Fernando Valley.

It covers:
- City of LA EWDD programs
- BusinessSource Centers
- JEDI Zones
- Business Response Unit
- LA Business Navigator
- Neighborhood Councils
- Chambers and business associations
- VICA, LAEDC, BizFed, Valley Economic Alliance, VEDC/VSEDC
- Notion import structure
- Figma frame map
- ACoolPROMPT for repeatable civic/economic-development scraping

Core rule: Rights -> Disclosure -> Proof.
