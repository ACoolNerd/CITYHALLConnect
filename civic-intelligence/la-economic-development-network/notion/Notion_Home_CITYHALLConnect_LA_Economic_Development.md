# CITYHALLConnect - LA Economic Development Network

## Databases to import

1. Economic Development Programs.
2. BusinessSource Centers.
3. JEDI Zones.
4. Neighborhood Council Ecosystem.
5. Regional Business Organizations.
6. Source Registry.

## Operating views

- By jurisdiction.
- By program type.
- By corridor.
- By council district.
- By Neighborhood Council.
- By chamber / association.
- By business support stage.
- By opportunity deadline.
- By confidence score.

## Governance

Every record must include source URL, scrape date, confidence score, rights status and review status.
